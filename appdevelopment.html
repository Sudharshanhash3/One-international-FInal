iOS

iOS (formerly iPhone OS) is a mobile operating system created and developed by Apple Inc. exclusively for its hardware. It is the operating system that presently powers many of the company's mobile devices, including the iPhone, iPod, and iPod Touch. Mobile operating systems like iOS differ from most other operating systems because they put each app in its own protective shell.
