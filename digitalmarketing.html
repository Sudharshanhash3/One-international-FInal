Pay Per Click

A business model whereby a company that has placed an advertisement on a website pays a sum of money to the host website when a user clicks on the advertisement. Pay-per-click. Pay-per-click ( PPC ), also known as cost per click ( CPC ), is an internet advertising model used to drive traffic to websites, in which an advertiser pays a publisher (typically a search engine, website owner, or a network of websites) when the ad is clicked.
